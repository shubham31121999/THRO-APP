Types of Fire Extinguishers

Water and Foam

Effective against Class A fires involving ordinary combustibles like wood and paper.

Dry Chemical

Can be used on Class A, B, and C fires. Commonly used in commercial and industrial settings.

Carbon Dioxide (CO2)

Works on Class B and C fires by displacing oxygen or removing heat from the fire.

ABC Multipurpose

Effective on Class A, B, and C fires. Suitable for homes and offices.
